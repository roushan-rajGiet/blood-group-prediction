Global Blood Bank Information

Essential facts and compatibility charts for blood donation and transfusion, supporting emergency preparedness.

Universal Donor

O Negative (O-)

Can be given to almost anyone with any blood type. Crucial for emergency transfusions when a patient's blood type is unknown.

Universal Recipient

AB Positive (AB+)

Can receive blood from any other blood type, both Rh positive and negative.

Always In Demand

O and B

These two groups are the most commonly needed, especially O negative due to its universal donor status.
